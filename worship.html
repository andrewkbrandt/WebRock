Worship

Worship time and Location
Sundays - 10:00 a.m.
The Rock Lutheran Church, 237 S. 3rd St, Seward, NE 68434
Everyone is welcome. Come as you are — jeans, shorts or t-shirts are fine. Enjoy a cup of coffee and some cookies. Experience worship in a new way through music, video & drama. Bring the kids — they will take an active part in our gatherings. Come relax, learn and celebrate with us!
Our cafe and worship area are on the third floor. Entrances are on both the north and south sides of the building and an elevator is available on the south side of the building. Bathrooms are in the hallway behind the worship area.
Bringing kids with you? We have a tub with coloring sheets located in the back of the worship area, a cry room in the back corner, and a space with toys for “restless rockers” to be taken out for playtime if needed. But don’t feel like you need to take the kids out of the service! We love having children of all ages in worship with us, even if they’re fussy!
If worship is cancelled due to weather, that information will be shared through our newsletter mailing list, on our Facebook page, and under the News tab on this website.
Contact us at info@therockseward.org with any questions about our worship services, activities, or beliefs.
